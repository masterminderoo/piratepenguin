Privacy Policy

Pirate Penguin Budapest (referred to as “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy.

1. Who We Are

The data controller is: Pirate Penguin Budapest
Budapest, Hungary
Contact: captain@piratepenguinbudapest.com

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data when you use this site or book a tour:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Payment details (processed via external services)
  • Communication history

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics tools at this time.

3. Why We Collect Your Data

We process your data to:

  • Handle bookings and payments
  • Communicate with you regarding your tour
  • Comply with tax or legal obligations

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not share your data with third parties unless:

  • Required by law
  • Necessary for payment processing (e.g. Revolut or PayPal)

We do not sell or rent your data.

5. How Long We Store Your Data

We retain booking-related data for the time required by Hungarian tax law (typically 5 years). Emails may be stored for communication reference.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Hungarian Data Protection Authority (NAIH)

7. Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, email us at: captain@piratepenguinbudapest.com
